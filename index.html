BLT Quality Meal at a Quality Price

Here at BLT we pride ourselves on using eco friendly practices and materials. We are situated on the Quays in Dublin City Center.We have been in the business since 1935 after the founding fathers of our great restaurant set up in New York City. We use purely Irish products and are proud to be an Irish Business. In 2010 we opened a second branch of our restaurant smack dab in the middle of Dublin City Centre and the business has been bustling since we opened our doors!
